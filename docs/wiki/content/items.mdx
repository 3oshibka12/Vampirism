Blood Bottle are used to store blood to drink or transport.

Glass Bottles are automatically transformed into Blood Bottles and back when they are filled/emptied.

<details><summary>disable autoconvert</summary>

You can disable the automatic conversion of Glass Bottles to Blood Bottles by setting `autoConvertGlassBottles` in the common config to `false`. Then you must craft the bottles.

</details>
