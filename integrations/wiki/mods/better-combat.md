## Features
### Weapon Attributes

The following Weapons have been given Weapon Attributes:
- Heart Seeker -> Claymore
- Heart Striker -> Claymore
- Hunter Axe -> Heavy Axe
- Pitchfork -> Spear
- Stake -> Soul Knife
